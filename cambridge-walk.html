A Cambridge Economics Walk

Very much a preliminary sketch of what could be an Economics Walk, probably more a hike, through Cambridge. At the instigation of Carolina Alves, then the Joan Robinson Fellow at Girton, and the encouragement of others, I started to put together this outline. Even in a very unfinished state it has proved useful and I have put it here with the hope it might continue to be improved and prompt some further suggestions!
